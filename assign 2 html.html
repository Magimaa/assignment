About

As a little girl living in mangalore, i have always leaned into drawing in my free time. Drawing has always been very instinctual, a form of meditation in a way.

I have always been a person who loves to observe the details, the small changes in the shades that create depth, on how an white dot could bring an eye alive, and all the little things that make a change.

My style: I’ve always been taken by works of realism and motivated by the challenge highly detailed subjects present.

My medium: Pencils remain my favourite tool because of the control, precision and line quality they allow, plus they’re clean to work with.

Skills:

  • Know about the drawing edges

  • Set the right proportions and sizes

  • Know different colour schemes

  • Understand the Concept of light and shadow

  • Convey clear perspective.
